Introduction

English is a universal language that is spoken around the world, even foreign people know how to speak atleast some english!

What is English day?

English day is a day for the
commemoration of the birth and death
of William Shakespeare (Mostly
celebrated in April 23) who was born
in 1564 April 23 and died in 1616 April 23
(Exact date of death is unknown).If you don't
know this guy, you probably know his famous
poem:"To be or not to be.".

Fun facts!

The earliest form of English is called Old English or Anglo-Saxon. And there are 170,000 words in the Oxford English Dictionary! The word "set" has the most definitions. The sentence "He believed Ceasar could see people seizing the seas" has all of the ways to spell the sound 'ee' (seven ways)! Isn't that cool?

Fun English games

First try playing wordle, which is a great game to test your vocabulary in English! Second, try to play ridella ,this game has fun English riddles. Lastly, try playing crossword, this game also tests your vocabulary but in a unique way!
